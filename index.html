Visit Dahanu

Enjoy the serene Dahanu-Bordi beach, explore abundant chikoo (sapota) orchards

Top three activities to do at dahanu

Relax on Dahanu beaches

Spend time at the calm, clean sands of Dahanu Beach or nearby Bordi Beach for swimming.

Visit Temples and Forts

Seek blessings at the prominent Mahalaxmi Temple and explore the historical Dahanu Fort.

Tour Chikoo Orchards wadi

Take a farm tour to see how the famous Dahanu chikoo (sapodilla) is grown and enjoy fresh fruits.
